Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know before you download.

Is CipherWatch safe? Does it replace Windows Defender?

No — CipherWatch works alongside Windows Defender, not instead of it. It never modifies or replaces Defender. It only reads your security status and launches built-in Windows security commands that already exist on your PC. Your core protection stays exactly the same.

What data does CipherWatch collect?

None. All your security data stays on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your personal information, scan results, or system data. CipherWatch is entirely local — it reads from Windows, displays to you, and stops there.

How does the AI assistant work?

You connect your own Claude API key in CipherWatch Settings. When you ask the AI a question, the query goes from your device directly to Anthropic's servers — not through us. We never see your questions or answers. You control the key, you control the data.

What's the difference between Pro and Ultimate?

Both include the full feature set: Quarantine Viewer, System Health Monitor, and the AI Assistant. Pro covers 1 device with 20 AI queries/month. Ultimate covers 3 devices with 60 AI queries/month and priority support — ideal for families or small businesses.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, absolutely. Pro and Ultimate plans come with a 14-day no-questions-asked refund. If CipherWatch isn't right for you, just reach out within 14 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full — no hoops to jump through.
